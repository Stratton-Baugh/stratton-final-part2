About Me

This is what I do.

I am a Utah native, deeply influenced by the state's exceptional access to the outdoors. My passion for nature drives my enthusiasm for a variety of outdoor activities, with a particular focus on fly fishing, skiing, and trail running. As the youngest of four siblings, with three older sisters, I value the importance of family and prioritize spending quality time with them. These experiences have shaped my character and work ethic, fostering a strong appreciation for both adventure and meaningful connections. I also love to travel to see new places and cultures with friends and family while also going on adventures.
